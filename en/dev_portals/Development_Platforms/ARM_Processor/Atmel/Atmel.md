#### sama5d4-xplained:

How to flash ?

-   1/ short jumper : BOOT\_DIS
-   2/ press reset
-   3/ open jumper
-   4/ time sudo \$(which sam-ba) "/dev/ttyACM1" "at91sama5d4x-ek"
    "\$tcl"
-   5/ hit reset again
